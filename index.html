History and Culture of Angkor Wat

Angkor Wat was built in the 12th century as a Hindu temple and later became a Buddhist site. It shows the rich culture of the Khmer Empire with its amazing architecture and detailed carvings. Today, it is a symbol of Cambodia’s history and pride.
